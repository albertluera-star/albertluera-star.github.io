ABOUT

WHO I AM

I'm a graphic designer based in Oakland, California, specializing in bold, eye-catching visuals that refuse to be ignored. My work combines the raw energy of punk aesthetics with contemporary design principles to create graphics that demand attention.

With a background in event promotion and social media marketing, I understand what it takes to make designs that not only look incredible but actually drive results. Every flyer, every post, every brand identity is crafted to stop the scroll and make an impact.

WHAT I DO

My specialty is creating visuals for the culture: event flyers that get people excited, social media graphics that spark engagement, and brand identities that capture the essence of what makes a business unique.

I work with venues, promoters, independent artists, local businesses, and anyone who needs designs with personality and punch. Whether it's a packed show at a underground venue or a social media campaign for a growing brand, I bring the same energy and attention to detail to every project.

MY APPROACH

I believe great design should be fearless. It should take risks, challenge conventions, and create genuine connections with audiences. I'm inspired by the DIY spirit of punk culture, the vibrancy of Oakland's creative scene, and the endless possibilities of digital design.
